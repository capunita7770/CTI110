Our History

Adrian Pinoy Cuisine is a family owned and operated business. In 2017 we established our first restaurant in Raeford, North Carolina. Adrian Pinoy Cuisine became higly recognized within the community through the introduction of our Filipino traditional food. We serve our entree hot and out of the stove freshly cook, custumers likely to order our special Lumpia and Adobo with rice.
